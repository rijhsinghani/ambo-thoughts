About Deacon Henry

"My Ambo Thoughts"

Deacon Henry Cugini is an ordained Roman Catholic Permanent Deacon who ministers in the Diocese of Trenton. He was ordained by Bishop Smith on May 8, 2008 and is assigned to the Co-Cathedral of St. Robert Bellarmine in Freehold, New Jersey.

His past and present ministries include Spiritual Direction, Annulment Advocate, Hospital and Homebound Ministries and Bereavement Ministry. He is a certified Spiritual Director, Pastoral Counselor and Bereavement Counselor/Facilitator. Deacon Henry is also a professed member of Third Order of St. Francis (Secular Franciscan).

Motto:

"Preach the Gospel at all times and if necessary use words…"
