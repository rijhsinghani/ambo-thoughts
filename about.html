About Deacon Henry

Deacon Henry Cugini

Diocese of Trenton, New Jersey

Deacon Henry Cugini serves the faithful of the Diocese of Trenton, bringing the Gospel message to life through preaching, teaching, and acts of charity. Ordained as a permanent deacon, Henry is dedicated to bridging the sacred and the everyday — meeting people where they are and walking alongside them in their faith journey.

Ministry

As a deacon, Henry's ministry spans three pillars: the Word, the Liturgy, and Charity. He regularly preaches at Sunday Mass, leads Bible study groups, and serves in outreach ministries that support the poor and marginalized. His homilies are known for their warmth, practical wisdom, and deep scriptural grounding.

The Diocese of Trenton

The Diocese of Trenton serves the Catholic faithful across Burlington, Mercer, Monmouth, and Ocean counties in central New Jersey. Established in 1881, the diocese encompasses over 100 parishes and missions, serving a diverse and vibrant Catholic community.

About This Site

Ambo Thoughts takes its name from the ambo — the stand from which Scripture is proclaimed and the homily is preached. This site extends that ministry beyond the parish walls, sharing reflections, homilies, and video content for anyone seeking to deepen their faith.

The site is managed by Sameer Rijhsinghani, a friend and fellow member of the parish community.
