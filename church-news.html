Vatican

Pope Leo XIV Releases Lenten Message for 2026

February 5, 2026

Pope Leo XIV's first Lenten message, entitled "Listening and Fasting," urges Catholics to listen more closely to God and others and to "disarm" their language by fasting from words that wound.

Vatican Meets with SSPX on Episcopal Consecrations

February 12, 2026

The Vatican received representatives of the Priestly Society of St. Pius X following the group's announcement of plans to consecrate new bishops. The meeting was led by Cardinal Víctor Manuel Fernández at the Dicastery for the Doctrine of the Faith.

Harvard Professor Appointed to Pontifical Academy

February 16, 2026

Pope Leo XIV appointed Tyler J. VanderWeele, Harvard epidemiology professor and director of the Human Flourishing Program, to the Pontifical Academy of Social Sciences.

Catholic Men for Jesus Christ Conference — Feb. 28

January 12, 2026

The 29th annual conference invites all Catholic men to a day of faith, fellowship, and inspiration at St. Mary Mother of God Church, Middletown. Theme: "Spiritual Warriors for Christ," featuring speakers Doug Barry, Fr. Glenn Sudano CFR, and Coach Greg Schiano.

Parishes Host Holy Hours for Peace

February 11, 2026

Bishop David M. O'Connell, C.M., informed pastors of the USCCB's appeal to hold Holy Hours for Peace across the nation. Multiple parishes throughout the diocese have scheduled Holy Hours in February and March.

The Boss Meets "The Boss"

Bruce Springsteen and Bishop David M. O'Connell, C.M., enjoyed an impromptu lunch at Federici's Restaurant in Freehold, arranged by Father Brian Butch of St. Anselm Parish, Tinton Falls.
